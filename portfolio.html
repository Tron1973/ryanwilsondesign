Claireified Healing Arts

Website www.claireifiedhealingarts.com

I designed this multipage informational website to drive more clients to their small business. This is their first website, and it has helped them grow their client base. This site is coded exclusively in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with no frameworks.

Charcoal Ed

Website www.charcoaled.epizy.com

This is the website I designed for the band Charcoal Ed. The site includes mp3 files of all their music for free download and streaming. This site uses HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
