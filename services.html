Custom Hand-Coded Web Design & Development

What I do differently as an independent web developer is that I hand-code and write custom websites myself, line by line. This allows a level of control and customization over the design not found on template-based platforms. As a result, the websites I build are fast and lean, loading and running at much faster speeds because the code is optimized for your site. Custom hand-coded websites perform at faster speeds with superior loading times compared to sites created with page builders and template sites. Faster speeds keep visitors on the site longer, and hold attention spans better.
